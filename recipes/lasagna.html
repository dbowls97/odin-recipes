Bleak and Bland Lasagna Recipe

Not as bad as it looks!

Tasteless garbage the whole family is sure to hate!

Ingredients:

  • Tomato paste
  • Ketchup
  • Wimpy Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Absolutely NO spice or seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Sautee beef on medium heat
  4. Empty ketchup and tomato paste into 12x8in baking dish
  5. Add your noodles and beef to the baking dish
  6. Yeet it into the oven for an hour or until the top is blackened.
